# AI Compliance Evidence Collection Kit

> **Version:** 1.0 | **Last updated:** 2026-03-14
> **Purpose:** Complete set of templates to gather compliance evidence for AI/LLM projects across global jurisdictions. Hand completed templates to a legal or compliance expert to help prove compliance.

## Quick Start

1. **Start with Template 21 (Jurisdiction Selector)** — check which countries you operate in
2. **Complete Template 17 (Risk Classification)** — determine your system's risk level
3. **Follow the jurisdiction selector output** — it tells you exactly which templates to complete
4. **Track deadlines with Template 22** — never miss a compliance date

## Template Conventions

Every template includes:
- **Metadata block** — session/GitHub ref, upload date, author, applicable laws
- **Laws served** — specific legislation this template provides evidence for
- **Evidence checklist** — at the bottom, confirming what has been gathered
- **Version history** — audit trail of changes

## How to Hand Off to Legal/Compliance

1. Complete all templates relevant to your jurisdictions
2. Ensure every template has metadata filled (session ref, date, author)
3. Package templates with the Evidence Matrix (`Law-Evidence-Matrix.md`)
4. Include the Jurisdiction Selector showing your specific requirements
5. Include the Deadline Tracker with your project-specific dates
6. Note any gaps or uncertainties for legal review
7. **Disclaimer:** These templates gather evidence — a qualified legal professional must review for compliance sign-off
