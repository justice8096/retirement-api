## 2. Disclosure Copy Templates

### 2.1 AI Interaction Notice (Chatbot / Conversational AI)

**Placement:** Before or at the start of interaction
**Jurisdictions:** EU, South Korea, Utah

> **Suggested copy:**
> "You are interacting with an AI assistant. A human representative is available upon request."

| Variation | Use Case |
|-----------|----------|
| Short | "Powered by AI" |
| Standard | "This conversation is with an AI assistant, not a human." |
| Detailed | "You are interacting with [System Name], an AI assistant developed by [Company]. This system uses [brief description]. You may request a human representative at any time by [action]." |

### 2.2 AI-Generated Content Label

**Placement:** On or adjacent to AI-generated text, images, audio, video
**Jurisdictions:** EU, China, South Korea, California SB 942

> **Suggested copy (visible):**
> "AI-Generated" or "Generated by AI" or "[Content type] created with AI assistance"

| Content Type | Label Format |
|-------------|-------------|
| Text | Inline label or footer: "This text was generated by AI." |
| Images | Overlay watermark or caption: "AI-Generated Image" |
| Audio | Pre-roll announcement or metadata tag |
| Video | On-screen persistent label + metadata |

**China-specific implicit metadata requirements:**
- Provider name
- Content attributes (type, generation method)
- Content number (unique identifier)
- Must be embedded in file metadata, not just visible

### 2.3 Automated Decision Disclosure

**Placement:** Before or alongside the decision output
**Jurisdictions:** Colorado, NYC, UK, South Africa

> **Suggested copy:**
> "This [decision type] was made with the assistance of an automated system. You have the right to [request human review / contest this decision / request an explanation]. Contact [method] for more information."

### 2.4 Employment AI Notice

**Placement:** Before AI is used in hiring/assessment
**Jurisdictions:** NYC LL 144, Illinois, Ontario

> **Suggested copy:**
> "We use an AI-assisted tool in our [screening/interview/assessment] process. This tool evaluates [what it evaluates]. An independent bias audit of this tool was conducted on [date] and is available at [URL]. You may request an alternative process by contacting [contact]."

### 2.5 AI Video Interview Notice (Illinois-specific)

**Placement:** Before video interview begins
**Jurisdictions:** Illinois AI Video Interview Act

> **Required elements:**
> 1. Notice that AI will be used to analyze the video interview
> 2. Description of how the AI works and what characteristics it evaluates
> 3. Explicit consent obtained from candidate (must be recorded)
> 4. Information about data retention and destruction timelines

### 2.6 Emotion Recognition / Biometric Notice

**Placement:** Before system activation
**Jurisdictions:** EU Art. 50

> **Suggested copy:**
> "This system uses [emotion recognition / biometric categorization]. It analyzes [what it analyzes] for the purpose of [purpose]. You may opt out by [action]."
